Support for Families Navigating Mental Health Challenges

Positive Thoughts to Hold On To

  • You Are Not Alone: Mental illness affects millions. Support is out there.
  • You Can Make a Difference: Your love and support matter deeply.
  • There Is Hope: Treatment and recovery are possible.
  • Self-Care Matters: Taking care of yourself helps you care for others.

Steps to Take as a Family Member

  • Educate yourself about the illness
  • Create a safe, supportive environment
  • Recognize early warning signs
  • Develop a crisis plan
  • Encourage treatment and stay involved
  • Set loving boundaries
  • Find support for yourself

Symptoms to Watch For

Mania (Bipolar Disorder)

  • Racing thoughts, inflated self-esteem
  • Risky behavior, little sleep, fast talking

Depression

  • Hopelessness, fatigue, isolation
  • Sleep or appetite changes, suicidal thoughts

Schizophrenia

  • Delusions, hallucinations, disorganized thinking
  • Emotional flatness, social withdrawal
